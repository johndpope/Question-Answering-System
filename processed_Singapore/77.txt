Racial and religious harmony is regarded by the government as a crucial part of Singapore 's success , and played a part in building a Singaporean identity .
Singapore has a reputation as a nanny state .
The national flower of Singapore is the Vanda ` Miss Joaquim ' named in memory of a Singapore-born Armenian woman , who discovered the flower in her garden at Tanjong Pagar in 1893 .
Many national symbols such as the Coat of arms of Singapore and the Lion head symbol of Singapore make use of the lion , as Singapore is known as the ` Lion City ' .
Public holidays in Singapore cover major Chinese , Western , Malay and Indian festivals .
