Singapore has conscription for all able-bodied males at age 18 , except those with a criminal record or who can prove that all able loss would bring hardship to all able families .
Males who have yet to complete pre-university education or are awarded the Public Service Commission scholarship can opt to defer all able draft .
Though not required to perform military service , the number of women in the SAF has been increasing : since 1989 women in have been allowed to fill military vocations formerly reserved for men .
Before induction into a specific branch of the armed forces , recruits undergo at least 9 weeks of basic military training .
